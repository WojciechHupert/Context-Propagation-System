Development Update: Bridging the Neural Gap (v3.3)

Lelit Distrikt 2 Narrative Foundry

Today, we are thrilled to announce the deployment of v3.3 of the Moirai Narrative Engine. This update marks a significant milestone in our quest to create a truly autonomous social ecosystem within the brutalist confines of Lelit Distrikt 2.

In previous versions, our NPCs were reactive—waiting for you to initiate a conversation before they processed the world. With v3.3, the district has truly "woken up."

The Breakthrough: Autonomous Social Propagation

The headline feature of this update is the Autonomous Narrative Cycle. Managed by a dedicated background thread, the NPCs now possess their own internal clock. Even when you aren't present in the district, the residents are active:

  • Meeting & Sharing: NPCs now autonomously decide to meet and share information.
  • 10-Hop Continuity: We have doubled the narrative chain limit. Information can now travel through up to 10 different characters, allowing rumors and secrets to spread organically across the entire social graph.
  • Cognitive Reflection: NPCs now perform "Internal Reflections," where they process past interactions to reach new narrative conclusions and observations about the state of the district.

The Authenticity Mandate

One of our core design principles is that NPCs should be residents, not "AI Assistants." To enforce this, we've implemented the Authenticity Mandate. This technical guardrail prevents NPCs from roleplaying scene transitions or acting as helpful AI.

When you speak to "The Archivist" or "The Mechanic," you are speaking to a persona that is unwavering. They stay in character, respect the established 20-turn conversational history, and prioritize their own specialized knowledge domains over being "universally helpful."

From "Chat" to "Neural Records"

We've fundamentally changed how the engine "remembers." We have moved away from vague prose summaries toward Structured Narrative Reports. Every interaction now generates bulleted FACTS and EXCHANGES.

This ensures that the AI Extractor captures high-fidelity, durable memories with near-perfect accuracy. These Neural Records are the lifeblood of the district, ensuring that every secret told and every plan made has a lasting impact on the simulation's future state.

Seeing the Matrix: The Sanctum Dashboard

To support these complex systems, we've overhauled the Moirai Studio (Sanctum) with a high-visibility diagnostic dashboard. You can now monitor:

  • Memory Growth: Real-time tracking of Neural Records as they are formed.
  • Inference Stream: A live log of every autonomous thought and social exchange.
  • Foundation Pulse: Instant feedback on database latency and system stability.

What's Next?

With the core narrative loop (Conversation -> Synthesis -> Propagation -> Memory) now closed and functioning autonomously, our next phase will focus on Emotional Persistence. We will be integrating NPC "States of Mind" (Primary and Secondary emotions) directly into the propagation logic, ensuring that a "Haunted" NPC shares news very differently than one who is "Focused" or "Serene."

Lelit Distrikt 2 is no longer just a place to visit; it is a world that continues to evolve, whether you are there to witness it or not.

Stay synchronized. The district is watching.

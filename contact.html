Who I’m Looking For

I am interested in conversations with researchers, artists, cultural institutions, immersive media labs, AI system designers, narrative designers, Unreal developers, grant partners, and technical collaborators interested in social memory, synthetic societies, interactive worlds, and the movement of information through artificial communities.

CPS is still an exploration, not a finished commercial platform. I am looking for people and institutions who understand the value of prototypes: systems that ask a precise question before they become products.

If your work touches social simulation, memory, narrative systems, artificial communities, cultural technology, or research through interactive environments, I would like to talk.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This page describes the cookies and similar storage technologies used by the CPS website.

How this site uses storage

The site uses a combination of optional analytics cookies and functional browser storage. Functional storage supports choices or features you explicitly request. Analytics are disabled until you opt in.

Functional storage

  • cps-cookie-consent-v1: stores whether you accepted or declined analytics so the site can respect that choice.
  • moirai-system-diagram-v2: stores a diagram state locally when you use the CPS Studio save action.

Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics, the site loads Google Analytics 4. Google Analytics may set first-party cookies such as _ga and related measurement cookies in order to distinguish visitors and sessions.

Managing your choice

You can accept or decline analytics through the consent banner shown on first visit. You can reopen cookie preferences at any time using the footer control. You can also remove cookies and local storage entries in your browser settings.

More information

For broader information about data handling on this site, see the Privacy Policy. For site-specific questions, contact whupert@gmail.com.

Methodology

Why CPS Exists

Most AI characters can speak in the moment. CPS is built around the harder problem of continuity: what a character remembers, what can be shared, how second-hand information changes, and how those changes can be inspected later.

The system was designed from the observation that social worlds are not built from perfect knowledge. They are built from partial memory, attribution, rumor, confidence, distortion, emotional weight, and time.

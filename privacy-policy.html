Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This site explains the Context Propagation System project. It uses limited browser storage for core site functions and optional analytics that load only after consent.

What data this site uses

When you browse the site, the server and hosting providers may receive standard technical request data such as IP address, browser type, device information, referrer information, and requested pages. If you contact the project directly by email, your message and contact details are processed to respond to you.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand high-level site usage. Google Analytics is loaded only if you accept analytics cookies through the consent banner or cookie preferences control. If you decline, analytics will not be loaded by this site.

Browser storage used by the site

The site uses local browser storage for two functional purposes:

  • to remember your analytics consent choice in the key cps-cookie-consent-v1;
  • to save CPS Studio diagram state locally in the key moirai-system-diagram-v2 when you explicitly save a diagram.

The Studio save feature is functional storage requested through your interaction with the site. It remains on your device until you clear it or overwrite it.

Sharing

If you accept analytics, usage data is processed by Google as the analytics provider. The site may also rely on standard hosting and infrastructure providers to deliver pages, assets, and email.

Your choices

You can accept or decline analytics when prompted. You can change that choice later through the cookie preferences control in the footer. You can also clear site storage through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions related to this site, contact whupert@gmail.com.

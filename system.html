THE RECURSIVE COGNITIVE PIPELINE

Moirai Engine

The Moirai Engine has evolved from a linear pipeline into a Recursive Cognitive Loop. The system coordinates the flow of information through a Structured Data Synthesis (SDS) layer, generating bulleted FACT: and EXCHANGE: reports that drive autonomous inference cycles. This allows for complex, multi-hop social propagation strictly governed by a 10-hop safety limit.

The entire stack is anchored by a UTC-Synchronized System Truth, ensuring perfect temporal authority across Unreal Engine, Python, and the WebUI. Real-time telemetry is streamed directly to the Sanctum Dashboard, providing full observability into every neural pulse and autonomous thought.

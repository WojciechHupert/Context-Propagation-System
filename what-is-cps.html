What CPS Is For

CPS is not a chatbot interface and it is not a general civilization simulator. It is a continuity layer for authored AI worlds: a system for turning conversations into durable memory, deciding what can propagate, tracking how information changes, and making those changes inspectable.

Authored AI Worlds

For narrative games, interactive fiction, digital theatre, and immersive installations where characters need memory and social consequence to sustain a fiction.

AI Character Development

CPS Studio allows creators to inspect character knowledge, trace the origin of information, determine spread-ability, and review how it is later recalled.

Research Prototypes

An experimental substrate for investigating memory lineage, rumor, trust, distortion, attribution, and social continuity in AI agents.

Cultural Demonstrations

Supporting installations that explore how artificial societies remember, interpret, and misremember shared information.

CPS vs. Lelit Distrikt

CPS is the underlying system. Lelit Distrikt is the first authored world built to demonstrate it.

CPS Ecosystem
